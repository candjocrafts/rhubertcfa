About

I build long-term partnerships with financial institutions at the intersection of markets, data, and technology.

Over nearly three decades, I have held leadership roles across global banks and market infrastructure providers, spanning executive support, project management, trading and risk, client onboarding, enterprise resiliency, and data & analytics. Today at Snowflake, I focus on helping financial institutions harness cloud-based data platforms and AI to modernize their operating models and unlock new sources of growth.

Previously at BNY Mellon, I led business development for data and analytics solutions, serving asset managers, asset owners, banks, and broker-dealers. I partnered with clients on the design of cloud-based data management, performance, and accounting solutions, while also creating written and graphical sales materials and Python-based AI/ML demonstrations for client engagements.

Earlier in my career I held senior roles at J.P. Morgan, HSBC, and Bank of America, where I built and led global teams responsible for client onboarding, regulatory change, and complex front-to-back transformations in the capital markets businesses.
